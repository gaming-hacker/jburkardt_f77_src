SQUARE_SYMQ_RULE
Symmetric Quadrature Rules for Squares.

SQUARE_SYMQ_RULE is a FORTRAN77 library which returns symmetric quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 20, over the interior of the symmetric square in 2D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

The original source code, from which this library was developed, is available from the Courant Mathematics and Computing Laboratory, at http://www.cims.nyu.edu/cmcl/quadratures/quadratures.html ,
