biharmonic_2d

biharmonic_2d, a FORTRAN77 library which solves a version of the biharmonic equation in two dimensions, by Petter Bjorstad.

The equation has the form: 

     u    + 2*u    + u    + alpha*(u  +u  ) + beta*u = f(x,y)
      xxxx     xxyy   yyyy         xx  yy
