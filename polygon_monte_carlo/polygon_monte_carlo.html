POLYGON_MONTE_CARLO
Monte Carlo Estimate of Integrals Over Polygon in 2D

POLYGON_MONTE_CARLO is a FORTRAN77 library which applies a Monte Carlo method to estimate the integral of a function over the interior of a polygon in 2D.
